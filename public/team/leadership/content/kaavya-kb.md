Hello! I'm Kaavya, a second year attempting to major in Biology with a Chemistry and EVS minor.

Much like Dobby, my spirit animal, I tend to run away at the sight of humans and am quite wary of them :) I would much rather spend my time with animals.

However, this wasn't always the case. Growing up, I was fascinated by all life around me, but would keep a distance and still have reservations. I still remember being really scared of dogs, to the point I wouldn't cross a road if there was a dog, without someone accompanying me.

This all changed when two of the most precious babies came into my life, our two cats - Kala and Nala. We adopted them during the lockdown, and I can safely say my life has completely changed after them. They taught me empathy, kindness, compassion, respect, and mindfulness of all living beings around me. My cats have instilled such an important quality in my family, and now all of us make our decisions based on its consequences on the living being around us. I now have fights with people because I refuse to kill a fly, or have constant experimental ethics arguments with people in the Biology environment.

Pawsitive is such an amazing space, because it allows me to be my true self, while never compromising on my moral compass. It provides a platform to constantly engage in my core values of animal welfare, and interactions with like-minded people. I'm grateful to be a part of this space, and contribute in the little way we can towards the wellbeing of other lives.

But formal things aside, I get to play with dogs! :)
