Hello there! I'm Srishti, and I'm majoring in Psychology with double minors in Political Science and Media Studies. I am a pet-parent (no wait, pet-sister) to a thirteen-year-old pug, Max, who basically grew as I did. Admittedly, much like most other people in Pawsitive, I prefer the company of animals to that of human beings. That was perhaps why my parents decided to give me Max for my seventh birthday in the first place. While I have always run into people who like dogs (that's easy, they're pretty darn likeable), Pawsitive was a space where I met people who were willing to care for them as much as I did. So much so that we would get our hands dirty, crawl into gutters and under cars, only to be able to lure a scared little puppy out for their vaccinations. And I couldn't be more grateful for those experiences.
