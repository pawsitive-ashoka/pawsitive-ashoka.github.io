Hi, I'm Pia Rattan.
At 4, I used to refuse to sleep, except on the floor with my grandparents dog Prince. As long as I can remember I was desperate to adopt a dog of my own, however my parents said no for a long time.

During this time I used to go down to the dog park and befriend aunties whose dogs I wanted to play with, I'd take their numbers and call them up — when most kids were calling each other to hangout in the park I was calling my favourite dog parents. When I was allowed to go out of my colony I became friends with the stray dogs outside and went to feed them before and after school. When I discovered an injured squirrel in the park I brought him home and named him Squirrelly.

So honestly I've always been involved with animals and informally animal welfare. Paws gives me the opportunity to hang out with and take care of dogs and officially call it "work".
