I think I got into animal welfare because of the dogs in my community growing up. They were incredibly friendly, and unlike the usual "stray dog" stereotype, they were basically part of the neighborhood. My two Shih Tzus, Cluedo and Lego, grew up alongside them and got along with the colony dogs surprisingly well, which made the boundary between "pet" and "street dog" feel almost nonexistent to me. Because of that, caring about community dogs always felt very natural.
