I seemed to have misunderstood the prompt, and instead of writing a brief description of myself, I accidentally penned a haiku for my spirit dog, Dumbo:

Dumbo of Tapri
My lil cutie patootie
Old man ass baby.

Long before Dumbo, however, there was Pluto, my nine-year-old indie. When we first got him, I was the only one who was slightly afraid of him, which probably motivated him to prove his niceness to me in particular. He treated me like his baby, insisting on sitting on my lap or curling up beside me, despite never being nearly that affectionate with anyone else. His persistence paid off. Now I love all dogs. And blueberries. I also love blueberries. In fact, I loved them enough to name one of Coco's puppies Blueberry.
