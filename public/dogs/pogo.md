Pogo turned up at Gate 3 as a kind soul whose wisdom is stored in his fluffy coat. Although wary of us at first, he slowly warmed up and has become a father figure to Kaddu, imparting his wisdom to the young pup. With his almost golden eyes and gentle personality, Pogo tried to charm his way into Coco's heart — unfortunately, Sultan beat him to it. Despite not gaining Coco's affections, Pogo continued to guide Kaddu in his journey to adulthood. His wisdom is a source of comfort for the students as well — a hug from Pogo helps to relieve a week's stress.
