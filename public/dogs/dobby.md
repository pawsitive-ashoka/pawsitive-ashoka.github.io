Dobby is one of Sultan's only daughters and, fittingly, the puppy who resembles him the most. Often found napping under parked cars, she springs to life the moment she spots a familiar face, bursting up with unmistakable excitement. Dobby is highly vocal, expressing herself through constant whines and whimpers, both while asking for pets and while receiving them. That confidence, however, disappears at the vet's office, where she suddenly discovers incredible speed and an impressive talent for avoidance.
