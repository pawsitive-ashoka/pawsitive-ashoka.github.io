Cookie doesn't belong to any single territory or pack. Over time, he has moved freely across Pawsitive's jurisdiction, eventually settling near a small tapri run by a family who fondly call him "Kaalu." They keep an eye out for him, feed him eggs, and are quick to call for help whenever they sense something might be wrong. Cookie's lack of a fixed pack doesn't mean he lacks acceptance — he navigates spaces effortlessly, moving through different areas without provoking conflict, something of a flâneur in his own right.
