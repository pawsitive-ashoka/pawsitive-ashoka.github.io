Toto is instantly recognisable by his signature asymmetrical ears — his left ear flops down casually while the right stands alert, giving him a permanently stylish look. As if that weren't enough, his natural "eyeliner" is always on point, adding to his quiet charm. One of the friendliest dogs around, Toto's eyes visibly light up the moment he spots someone he recognises. Gentle and easygoing by nature, he prefers to keep to his own space and rarely involves himself in conflicts, moving through his surroundings with calm confidence.
