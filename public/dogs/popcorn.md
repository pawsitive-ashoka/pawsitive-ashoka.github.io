True to her name, Popcorn springs into the air like a popcorn kernel every time she sees someone she knows, bursting with uncontrollable excitement. At peak enthusiasm, she folds her front legs, lifts her back end, and eventually flops over belly-up, fully overwhelmed by joy. Easily distracted and endlessly affectionate, Popcorn insists on giving everyone equal attention, often choosing people over food without hesitation. Behind her bubbly energy, however, is a fiercely territorial dog who isn't afraid to stand her ground when needed.
