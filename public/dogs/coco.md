Coco is the fiercely protective mother of Kaddu and the undisputed queen of her territory. Though a devoted parent, she often behaves like a child herself — playful, free-spirited, and constantly seeking attention from her favourite humans. Coco has mastered the art of communication: she offers paw shakes without ever being taught, and if denied pets, will gently grab your hand with her paw to make her intentions very clear. Call her from a distance and she'll sprint toward you, launching herself into an enthusiastic hug. Fearless with unfamiliar dogs, Coco never hesitates to assert her authority.
