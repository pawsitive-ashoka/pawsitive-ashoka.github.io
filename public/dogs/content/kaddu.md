Kaddu may be best known as Coco's son, but within Pawsitive he's infamous for having the survival instincts of a tissue paper. A classic flight risk, Kaddu frequently sets off on ambitious quests to explore new territories and make new friends — only to end up thoroughly terrified of wherever he's landed. His signature look, a permanent blend of concern and confusion, accentuated by the wrinkles on his forehead, only adds to his charm. When Coco springs into action to defend her territory, Kaddu tries his best to join in, though it's often unclear whether he knows what the mission is.
