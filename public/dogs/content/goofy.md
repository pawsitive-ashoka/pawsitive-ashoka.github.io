Goofy lives up to his name with his friendly personality and general predisposition towards wagging his tail with everything he has to offer. When we found Goofy near ASG Coffee Shop, he had a huge cut on his side, but it didn't deter him from his goofy behaviour. While he is an expert in disappearing when he needs to be taken to the vet, he recovered well from the injury. Goofy doesn't care much about food — he can be seen having a bite of dinner, determining whether it is worthy of his exquisite taste buds and often dismissing it as unsatisfactory. He is never shy to display his constant love for pets.
