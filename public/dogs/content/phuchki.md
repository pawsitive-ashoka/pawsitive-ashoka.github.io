Phuchki (also known as Strawberry) is a quiet yet commanding presence — the kind of dog whose authority doesn't need to be announced. He rarely growls or snarls; instead, he sets clear boundaries simply by existing, making it instinctively obvious that his space should be respected. This calm dominance is recognised by everyone around him — even Brownie, the largest dog in the area, understands and honours Phuchki's quiet power. Steady, composed, and self-assured, Phuchki leads not through noise or force, but through an unspoken confidence that others naturally respond to.
