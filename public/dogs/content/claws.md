Claws is the perpetually exhausted mother of Barfi, Dobby, and Winky, and it shows. While she shares her children's playful and energetic nature, Claws is far more selective about how she spends her time — and with whom. A firm believer in conserving energy, she is often nowhere to be found until food makes an appearance. At that point, Claws re-emerges with impressive speed and focus, reminding everyone that while companionship is optional, snacks are non-negotiable. Practical, independent, and quietly spirited, she carries herself with the air of a mother who has done her time and knows exactly what she wants.
