Bunty is a quiet, timid dog from the same litter as Popcorn. He lives at Gate 1 with Boop and Stockings, though he hasn't fully found his place within the pack yet. His shy nature often makes him vulnerable to bullying, especially from Boop. Bunty takes time to warm up to people, but once he trusts you, he becomes a loyal little shadow, following you around and offering affection in his own soft, sincere way.
