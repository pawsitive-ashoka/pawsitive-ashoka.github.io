Hero was named after the bike we first found him hiding under — a Hero Honda. He had taken shelter there after a fight with Dumbo and Cookie that left the poor guy badly shaken. The incident made him extremely wary of people, and for a long time he would wail uncontrollably at even the slightest touch.

With patience, however, Hero has begun to show his softer side. Once he feels comfortable around someone, he truly enjoys their company. He expresses himself not only through small, excited tail wags but also through the movement of his oversized ears, which somehow seem even bigger than his face. And then there are his sweet, doll-like eyes — almost impossible not to fall in love with the moment you see them.
