Winky is another of Sultan's children, from the same litter as Barfi and Dobby, and resembles his father most in both size and temperament. That said, Winky marches very much to the beat of his own drum. He has a peculiar habit of attempting to jump over other dogs, often miscalculating and ending up awkwardly suspended above them. Lacking both grace and a clear exit strategy, these encounters usually conclude with someone — sometimes everyone — toppling over. Equal parts chaotic and endearing, Winky is impossible to ignore.
