Sid, named after the sloth from Ice Age for his striking resemblance to the character, is a distinguished little gentleman. He has a habit of offering his paw, often even before anyone asks for it. In fact, he does it so often that he has mastered the art of balancing comfortably on three legs while holding the fourth out expectantly.

However, all of that chivalry quickly disappears when food is involved. The moment Sid catches the scent of something he likes, the gentlemanly manners vanish. In an instant, he will pounce and use that very same paw to snatch the treat straight out of your hands.
