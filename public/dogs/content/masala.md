Masala is an older female dog at Gate 2 who wisely keeps herself above the constant power struggles between Sultan, Sutta, and Naruto. That distance is not a sign of weakness — when needed, Masala can more than hold her own and has been known to challenge all three without hesitation. Deeply fond of humans, Masala is expressive to a fault. She communicates her affection loudly and clearly through whines and whimpers that somehow always make perfect sense. With Masala, you're never left guessing how she feels — she makes sure you know.
