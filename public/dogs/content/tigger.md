Tigger is an endlessly excited little dog who always seems to be in motion. No matter what is happening around him, Tigger is somewhere in the middle of it — constantly bouncing around with a kind of boundless enthusiasm that makes it nearly impossible not to smile. He takes everything in stride and meets every moment as if it's the most exciting thing that has ever happened to him.

His signature move is a series of small, energetic little jumps, the kind that seem to say he simply cannot contain how happy he is right now. Even after all these years, the excitement hasn't dimmed one bit. Tigger is the kind of dog who reminds you that enthusiasm, handled with enough heart, never really gets old.
