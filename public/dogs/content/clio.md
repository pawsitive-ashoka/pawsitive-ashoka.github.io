Clio is a young dog and the mother of Batman, Wayne, Moshi, Toshi, and Napoleon. She is extremely timid and does not trust people easily, often avoiding food offered by those she is unfamiliar with.

Over time, however, Clio has slowly begun to trust the Pawsitive team. While she still does not allow us to pet her, she now feels comfortable enough to approach us for food. She has been getting healthier by the day, and we hope that, with time, she will grow more comfortable around people.
