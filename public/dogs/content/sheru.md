Sheru is a resilient little dog whose front legs, affected by rickets, give him a distinctive and slightly wobbly walk. The condition may shape the way he moves, but it has never dampened his spirit. Sheru is one of the most playful dogs you could hope to meet, always eager to greet people with excited little jumps — even if his body doesn't quite cooperate the way he intends.

Sheru is far from helpless. He has learned to stand his ground and make sure he isn't underestimated. In fact, when food is placed out for the pack, Sheru often manages to claim the lion's share for himself, proving that determination can sometimes matter far more than size or strength.
