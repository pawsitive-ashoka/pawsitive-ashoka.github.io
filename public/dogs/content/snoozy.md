Snoozy is a quiet, unobtrusive dog who can almost always be found sleeping in his favourite corner outside the ASG Coffee Shop and Restro. With his round, well-fed frame and his tongue perpetually lolling out, he has a delightfully peculiar appearance that makes him hard to miss. Easily startled and deeply non-confrontational, Snoozy keeps well away from any trouble. Content to nap the day away and mind his own business, he brings a calm, sleepy presence to the space he's made his own.
