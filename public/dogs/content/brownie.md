Brownie is a large, striking dog whose wolf-like features and Siberian Husky resemblance make him hard to miss — and, at first glance, a little intimidating. Despite his imposing build and powerful presence, Brownie is exceptionally friendly and people-oriented. His calm, approachable nature often surprises those who judge him by appearance alone. While his size can be daunting, those who take a moment to know him quickly realise that Brownie is gentle at heart — a dog whose strength is matched by warmth and quiet confidence.
