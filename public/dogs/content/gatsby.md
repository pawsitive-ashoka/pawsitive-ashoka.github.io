Gatsby is a calm and composed dog who carries himself with a quiet confidence. He is not particularly loud or excitable — in fact, he seems to prefer standing back and observing a situation before engaging with it. There is something almost unhurried about the way he moves through the world, as if he's seen enough of it to know that most things aren't worth rushing over.

What makes Gatsby stand out most is what happens when tensions rise. When conflict starts to brew around him, he doesn't shrink away or escalate things. Instead, he steps forward with a quiet determination, a steady presence you can't quite ignore. It's a quality that seems well-suited to his name — composed, deliberate, and with those striking light green eyes, a little enigmatic too.
