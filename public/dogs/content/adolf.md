Adolf, believed to be the leader of the Roti Boti pack, is often somewhat elusive. More often than not, he is found wandering beyond the pack's usual spots, frequently keeping company with the bulls that graze across their territory.

Though generally unbothered by most things around him, Adolf takes his role as a guardian seriously and is quick to make his disapproval known to unfamiliar visitors. Beneath that protective exterior, however, he is simply a big baby who enjoys being fussed over and loved.
