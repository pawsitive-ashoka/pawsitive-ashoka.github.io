Kenai, named after the protagonist of Disney's Brother Bear (2003), is a large, fluffy black dog. When he isn't lounging on the rooftop of his home — quite literally — he can usually be found wandering around in search of leftovers.

Kenai, frankly, couldn't care less about people or the attention they might offer him. His true interest lies in the food they may be carrying. He will patiently stand his ground nearby, keeping quiet watch until someone eventually gives in and offers him a morsel.
