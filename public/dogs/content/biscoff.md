Biscoff shares a very similar temperament to Julia — both timid and intensely curious about the people around him. He often lingers nearby, watching attentively as if trying to understand whether approaching would be a good idea. The desire for affection is obvious, but every attempt is accompanied by a quick retreat the moment things feel too close.

Like Julia, Biscoff has a habit of jumping back and forth in this uncertain rhythm: a step forward out of curiosity, then a quick hop away out of caution. It's a small but telling sign of a dog who wants to trust, and who may simply need a little more time to feel safe enough to do so.
