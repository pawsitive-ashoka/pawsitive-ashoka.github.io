Julia is a shy but deeply curious dog who seems to be constantly caught between fear and fascination. She watches people carefully from a short distance, clearly wanting to come closer but hesitating at the last moment. Whenever someone reaches out to her, Julia often takes a few tentative steps forward before quickly hopping back again, as if still deciding whether it is safe to trust.

This back-and-forth dance has become one of her most endearing habits. It's clear that Julia wants affection — she just hasn't quite worked up the courage to ask for it yet. With time and patience, it seems likely that her curiosity will slowly win over her fear.
