Caramel is a gentle, shy and slightly timid dog who can be found curled up in the tapri at the end of the road. In the past year, she has formed a beautiful bond with the bhaiya at the tapri, making her a recipient of unlimited biscuits and pets. When Caramel was initially at Gate 2, she was often subjected to bullying by the bigger dogs such as Sultan and Masala. However, she has come a long way now and is doing amazingly well at the tapri. Caramel's affections can be earned by providing pets and an ample supply of boiled eggs.
