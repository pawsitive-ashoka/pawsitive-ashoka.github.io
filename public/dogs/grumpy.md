Grumpy is often misunderstood, partly because of his name. He isn't constantly moody or stubborn — he's simply unpredictable. Like a teenager with dramatic mood swings, there are days when he seeks out affection, and others when he wants absolutely nothing to do with it. One thing, however, never changes: his love for food. If you're carrying snacks, especially something sweet, you're immediately his favourite person. Grumpy is known to occasionally go AWOL, preferring his independence, but make no mistake — when a threat appears, he shows up for his pack without hesitation.
