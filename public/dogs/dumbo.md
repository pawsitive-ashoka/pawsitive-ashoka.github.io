Dumbo isn't dumb — he's just perpetually confused. With a thoughtful, slightly puzzled expression, he often looks like he's quietly contemplating the meaning of life. That air of constant wonder only adds to his charm. Friendly and affectionate, Dumbo enjoys attention and greets familiar faces with gentle enthusiasm. While he's not usually the first to jump into conflict, he is reliably the second in command, often following Buzo into territorial disputes. Loyal and earnest, Dumbo may not always understand what's going on, but he's always present, committed, and ready to stand by his pack.
