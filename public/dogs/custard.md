Custard (also known as Martini) is a quietly remarkable dog. Extremely shy and deeply cautious, she does not allow people to approach or pet her, preferring to maintain a safe distance at all times. While the roots of her fear are unclear, it has taught those around her an important lesson in patience, respect, and loving from afar. Custard has chosen her own family on campus — she is Claws' constant companion and the self-appointed godmother to Barfi, Winky, and Dobby. Though reserved with humans, her loyalty and care for her pack speak volumes.
