Ice Cream is what happens when you max out the affection and physicality slider on a dog. He will make maximum body contact with you at any given moment — he doesn't like attention, he loves affection, and will push his snout as far into your stomach as caninely possible. He's a brave boy currently at odds with Sultan and his lackeys at Gate 2, giving that group of much larger dogs a tough fight. Ice Cream came to Ashoka with a bad case of mange, from which he has made a strong recovery.
