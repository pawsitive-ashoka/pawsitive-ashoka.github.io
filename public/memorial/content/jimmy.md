Jimmy was one of Ashoka's most beloved campus dogs, a constant and comforting presence for students and staff alike. He had a rare gentleness about him — patient, warm, and always happy to sit beside anyone who needed company. Whether it was a stressful exam week or a quiet evening at the tapri, Jimmy somehow always knew where he was needed most.

He was cared for by Pawsitive since the club's early days and became something of an unofficial mascot. His passing left a quiet in certain corners of campus that his admirers still feel. He is remembered with great love.
