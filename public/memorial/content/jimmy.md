Jimmy was a brown dog with white patches, estimated to be around two to three years old. She had recently given birth to a litter of puppies. While we did not know her for long, the fact that she had just become a mother reminds us that her life was tied to the quiet, everyday rhythms of the village she lived in.

We would like to extend our sincere thanks to Sulaiman, Haasan, Arbaaz, Ayaan, Numan, Sohil, and Vishal — a group of children from Asawarpur who helped us through the burial process and who were the ones to give Jimmy her name posthumously.
