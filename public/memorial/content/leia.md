Leia was a gentle dog who made Ashoka her home in June 2025. In the short time we knew her, she revealed herself to be both timid and affectionate, often greeting people with a soft wag of her tail once she felt safe.

Though her time on campus was brief, she left a quiet impression on those who cared for her. Leia fought until the very end, and for that, we are deeply proud of her.
