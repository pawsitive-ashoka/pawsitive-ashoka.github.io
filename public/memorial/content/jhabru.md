Jhabru was a black, white, and brown female puppy from the Claws litter. She was the largest and strongest of the four puppies, often giving her brothers, Odin and Voldemort, a run for their money — especially compared to her sister Gori, who was the runt of the litter.

The litter was first noticed and cared for by a shuttle bus driver, who also gave Jhabru her name. He told us that Jhabru, in his language, means "heart of a lion." The name suited her well. In the short time she was around, Jhabru was bold, confident, and full of energy. Whenever we brought puppy formula or eggs, she would always scramble her way to the very top of the puppy pile.
