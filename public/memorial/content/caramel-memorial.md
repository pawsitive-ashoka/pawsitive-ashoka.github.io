Caramel was a gentle, shy, and slightly timid dog who could be found curled up in the tapri at the end of the road. In the time she was with us, she formed a beautiful bond with the bhaiya at the tapri, making her a recipient of unlimited biscuits and pets.

When Caramel was initially at Gate 2, she was often subjected to bullying by the bigger dogs such as Sultan and Masala. However, she came a long way from those days and was doing remarkably well at the tapri. Caramel's affections could be earned by providing pets and an ample supply of boiled eggs.
