🗂️ our departments

four verticals, one mission — every angle of animal care covered

Pawsitive runs four specialised teams that together make up a full ecosystem of care, from the ground to the gram.
each vertical is essential. none of us work in silos.
VERTICAL 01
🎪

Events & Logistics

The creative engine. This team turns ideas into action — designing and executing campaigns that engage, educate, and inspire the campus community.

  • Ideating, planning & executing original events and campaigns independently and with other student groups
  • Leading at least one flagship event per semester alongside smaller thematic initiatives
  • Primary point of contact for external collaborators and workshop-based engagements
VERTICAL 02
💼

Finance & Outreach

The backbone of sustainability. This team ensures resources flow where they're needed most and builds the partnerships that keep the club running.

  • Managing all incoming finances — donations, event revenue — with accurate record-keeping
  • Ensuring funds are available for medical care, food & emergency needs; timely reimbursements
  • Outreach to companies, NGOs & aligned organisations for funding, resources & collaborations
VERTICAL 03
🐾

On-Ground & Feeding

The heart of Pawsitive. This team is on the ground every single day — feeding, monitoring, and responding to every animal that needs care.

  • Year-round feeding operations, coordinating student schedules including vacation periods
  • Responding to medical emergencies, maintaining stocked kits & medication schedules
  • Updated census of animals under care; coordinating all veterinary visits & follow-ups
VERTICAL 04
📱

Social Media & Marketing

The voice of Pawsitive. This team amplifies the club's mission online — building an audience that cares, advocates, and actually shows up.

  • Curating & posting content across all platforms — artwork, articles, commentary on relevant issues
  • Leading digital campaigns & ensuring consistent documentation of all club initiatives year-round
  • Managing the club's LinkedIn to build a credible professional profile for external collaborators
