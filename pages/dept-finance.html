Finance & Outreach

The backbone of sustainability.

what we do

Finance & Outreach is the team that keeps Pawsitive operationally alive. Without this vertical, nothing else runs. We manage every rupee that flows through the club — from donations to event revenue — and make sure that funds reach exactly where they're needed: emergency vet bills, food supplies, medication, and more.

But it's not just about the numbers. This team also builds Pawsitive's external relationships — reaching out to companies, NGOs, and aligned organisations for funding, sponsorships, and collaborative opportunities. We're the team that turns goodwill into concrete support.

responsibilities

  • Managing all incoming finances — donations, event revenue, grants — with transparent and accurate record-keeping
  • Ensuring funds are available for medical care, food & emergency needs at all times
  • Processing timely reimbursements for all club expenses across verticals
  • Outreach to companies, NGOs & aligned organisations for funding, resources & long-term collaborations
  • Drafting funding proposals and maintaining relationships with existing sponsors and donors
  • Producing termly financial summaries to keep the club accountable and transparent
