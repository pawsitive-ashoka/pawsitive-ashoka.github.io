Finance & Outreach

The backbone of sustainability.

what we do

This team manages all incoming finances, including donations and event revenue, while maintaining accurate records to ensure funds are readily available for medical care, food, and emergency needs. They oversee timely reimbursements for student expenses and lead outreach to companies, NGOs, and aligned organisations to secure funding, resources, and collaborative opportunities for the club.

responsibilities

  • Managing all incoming finances — donations, event revenue, grants — with transparent and accurate record-keeping
  • Ensuring funds are available for medical care, food & emergency needs at all times
  • Processing timely reimbursements for all club expenses across verticals
  • Outreach to companies, NGOs & aligned organisations for funding, resources & long-term collaborations
  • Drafting funding proposals and maintaining relationships with existing sponsors and donors
  • Producing termly financial summaries to keep the club accountable and transparent

who thrives here

You're financially literate and organised. You care deeply about how resources are allocated and you hold yourself to a high standard of accuracy. You're also personable and comfortable reaching out to organisations cold — you believe in the cause enough to pitch it convincingly to strangers.

support our work
