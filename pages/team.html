who we are

more than a club — a pack

We are students from different years, majors, and hostels — bound together by one belief: that every animal at Ashoka deserves someone in their corner. Some of us joined because of a dog we saw limping near gate two. Some because we'd always wanted to do something that mattered. All of us stayed because of the community we found here.
