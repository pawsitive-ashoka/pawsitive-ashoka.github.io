🐾 about pawsitive

We are a student-run animal welfare club at Ashoka University, built on the simple belief that every creature on this campus deserves kindness, safety, and care.

our story

how it all began

Pawsitive was born from a small group of students who noticed the campus dogs needed more consistent care. What started as informal feeding rounds grew into a full-fledged welfare club, with proper vet tie-ups, volunteer rosters, and a whole lot of love.

our mission

what drives us

To make Ashoka a safe and loving environment for every animal that calls it home. We believe in coexistence — that a thriving campus community includes its four-legged residents too.

our values

what we believe in

Compassion without conditions. Every dog, every cat, every creature matters — regardless of breed, background, or how scruffy they look. We show up for them, rain or shine.

get involved

join the pack

Whether you want to volunteer for feeding rounds, help with vet visits, organise awareness events, or just give belly rubs on tough days — there's a place for you in our pack.
