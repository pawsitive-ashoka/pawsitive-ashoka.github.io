📸 share your dog

send photos, videos, or artwork and your dog's story for a chance to be featured by the pawsitive team.

how this works

  1. Fill in dog and owner details.
  2. Upload images, videos, and artwork files.
  3. Our team reviews every entry before featuring.

By submitting, you confirm you have permission to share these files and allow pawsitive to repost selected entries with credit.

quick guidelines

  • Supported: JPG, PNG, WEBP, GIF, MP4, MOV, WEBM
  • Max file size: 10 MB each
  • Max total size: 100 MB per submission
  • Multiple files accepted

Team only: open review desk
